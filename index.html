How It Works

    
Three simple steps from upload to diagnosis report.

    

      

        

        
Upload MRI Image

        
Drag & drop your brain MRI scan in any standard image format. Our system accepts DICOM-derived images.

Our CNN model processes the scan, identifies patterns, and generates a GradCAM heatmap highlighting regions of interest.

      

      

        

        
Download Full Report

        
Get a detailed PDF report with prediction result, confidence score, heatmap overlay, and timestamp — ready to share.
